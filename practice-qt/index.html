Practice Qualifying Test

Before you start the tests, we recommend the following:

  • You use a tablet, laptop or desktop computer with an active and/or stable internet connection and a web browser.
  • You perform any software updates or upgrades.
  • You have a copy of these instructions to hand as they will not be available on screen after you begin the test.
  • You should use Google Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge or Safari browsers.
  • You should not use Internet Explorer as this does not support the test.
  • You should not take a test on a mobile phone as you may not be able to see everything clearly.
  • You should not use a government device as this may reduce the functionality of the test.
  • Some work devices may not provide full functionality of the test.
  • Once you start the test do not press the back button on your browser at any time.
  • You can move between the pages of the test by clicking on the previous and next buttons at the bottom of the test screen.
